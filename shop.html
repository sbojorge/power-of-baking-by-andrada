Layer Cakes

Cakes have been on my loop since I baked that fist one for my best friend's birthday in college.
Nowadays I'm happy to propose 3 main types of cakes:

  • Chiffon cake;
  • Redvelvet cake (maybe my favorite one);
  • Sponge cake.

Available decorations are:

  • Fresh berries (availability according to the season);
  • Whipped milk or dark chocolate ganache and mascarpone;
  • Drip chocolate;
  • Kinder bueno.

And for the filling:

  • Fruit mousse;
  • Mousseline cream;
  • Pastry cream;
  • White chocolate mousse...

Minimum order (for any type of cake): 2 Kg;


Price (decoration & filling of your choice included): 25€/Kg.

What is it a...


Chiffon cake: It's a very light cake made using vegetable oil instead of butter.


Red velvet cake: It's a rich cake with a subtle taste of chocolate granted by the cocoa that's one of its ingredients.


Sponge cake: It's a light cake that doesn't have any baking powder among its ingredients.


Mousseline cream: It's a rich cream prepared by adding butter to pastry cream (crème pâtissière)
