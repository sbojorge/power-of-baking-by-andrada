What is it a...


Chiffon cake: It's a very light cake that goes well with whipped creams, pastry cream and mousses. The cake will keep its beautiful texture even if refrigerated.


Red velvet cake: It's a rich, buttery, fluffy cake with a subtle taste of chocolate granted by the cocoa,one of its ingredients.


Sponge cake: It's a light, airy cake that's been leavened with beaten eggs instead of yeast. Perfect cake for "spongying" the taste of any topping.


Mousseline cream: It's a rich cream prepared by adding butter to pastry cream (crème pâtissière).
